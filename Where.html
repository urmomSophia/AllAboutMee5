Here Is Where I've Been and Where I Want To Go!

    Where I've Been:

  • Cancun, Mexico:

    • This was the first time that I went out of the country. Cancun, Mexico is for sure a tourist destination and because of that it makes sure you have a good time there. Me and my parents stayed at a hotel called The Royal Princess, and we had a whole pool to ourselves. The hotel also had a swimup bar and was right next to the beach, which would always be overcrowded.

  • Oskaloosa, Iowa:

    • Oskaloosa, Iowa is a beautiful place to find yourself amoung the wilderness. We went to visit my dad's host family from the first time he came to the United States. They have beautiful rolling hills and landscapes to amaze you. There is a quaint little shopping center in Oskaloosa named Pella with everything a woman could want from fancy resturants to boutiques. My dad used to play baseball here when he was in high school.

  • Chicago, Illinois:

    • Along with going to Iowa, we went to Illinois and of course if you go to Illinois, you should go to Chicago. We went for a family friend's wedding and they paid the hotel for all the guests. Our hotel was huge, right in the center of the city. Everything was at walking distance from Publix to Macy's. Most hotels had a major shop attached to it. Our hotel had a Publix attached to it.

Fun Fact:
Iowa is known for baseball, which is engraved into it's history after the Civil War with 6 MLB teams. This is the only state to have 6 MLB teams.

It just has beautiful scenery and beautiful sunrises and sunsets. Paris is one of the places I would love to go to.

The Colosseum is the landmark of Rome and I would love to take a picture there.

Their truely original gods and their stories will have you amazed and astonished by the original writings of Homer and Aristotle.
