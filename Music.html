All About My Music Interests!

In my 13 (almost 14!) years of life, music has helped me through tough times. Music heals the soul and can help one do anything! Feeling like hitting the gym? Play some pop to get you in the mood to move! Need to study? There is music for that too!
I will listen to any music genre so I don't really have a favorite one! I have been to one concert in my life when I was 10. It was a Chino y Nacho concert. They were a Spanish duo who broke apart recently and I went to their concert before that. If I were to choose someone to go watch, I would go to a Sebastian Yatra concert!
Musical instruments? I played the piano for a whole year before my electrical piano died and my parents still haven't gotten the batteries to this day.
